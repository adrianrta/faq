**Q: How long have you been playing [game]?**

**Q: Why Mario speedruns?**
> **A:** *"The movement is $wag money" -Twirly*

**Q: Why long categories?**
> **A:** The small room for error + a big PB in a long category > a small PB in a short category

**Q: Why do you take ages (re)learning a category?**
> **A:** I want to take my time for the long term; I improve way faster once I start runs
